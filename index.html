Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I schedule a grooming appointment?

The frequency of grooming appointments can vary depending on your pet's breed, coat type, and activity level. We recommend scheduling a grooming session every 4-6 weeks to keep your pet looking and feeling their best.

Do you offer any special services for senior or special needs pets?

Absolutely! Our experienced groomers are trained to handle senior pets and those with special needs with the utmost care and patience. We can customize our services to ensure a comfortable and stress-free experience.

What should I do to prepare my pet for the grooming appointment?

Prior to the appointment, please ensure your pet is well-rested and has been fed. It's also helpful to familiarize them with the grooming process by introducing them to the sounds and smells of the grooming tools at home.
